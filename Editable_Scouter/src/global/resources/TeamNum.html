Setting up the Team Numbers in Preparation Panel


The first three spinners under the Headings Red Alliance and Blue Alliance are places where you enter the team numbers. The first one under the Red Alliance heading denoted as R1, or red 1 and the second one  under the heading denoted as R2 or red 2, etc, it is the same for the spinners under the Blue Alliance Heading. When entering in the numbers make sure that you press enter after entering in the number to avoid any glitches/bugs from occurring. Furthermore if you do not want to fill it out manually see Loading Match Presets


Important

Ensure that there are no duplicate numbers and that all team numbers are filled, otherwise some weird things may happen.
