What to Do in Autonomous

Generally a team will only cross one defence in autonomous if any at all. To record this select the defence that the team crossed/reached in the array of different defences in the top portion of the game panel.

Afterwards press either the Autonomous defence reached/crossed buttons depending on whether the team crossed(got the robot completely across the defence) or reached(got the robot on the defence but not off of it i.e. stuck on it) in autonomous.

Furthermore if the team is really good and is able to score in high or low goals just note it down in the comment box.
